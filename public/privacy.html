Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

1. Overview

AfterFlow AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how AfterFlow AI collects, uses, and safeguards information when you install and use our Shopify application.

AfterFlow AI is a Shopify application that analyzes non-PII order metadata — specifically customer order notes — to automatically generate fulfillment tags and urgency assessments for merchants. We do not collect, store, or process personal customer data beyond what is necessary for this service.

Our core principle: AfterFlow AI processes only order-level metadata (customer notes) to generate non-PII fulfillment tags. We do not sell, share, or retain personal customer information.

2. What We Collect

When you install and use AfterFlow AI, we may collect the following data:

Data Type What It Is Why We Collect It
Order Notes Text entered by customers in the order note field AI analysis to generate fulfillment tags
Order IDs Shopify internal order identifiers Idempotency, logging, tag writing
AI Analysis Results Tags, urgency level, and summary text Stored in your database for dashboard display
Store Domain Your Shopify store's myshopify.com domain Authentication and API scoping

3. What We Do NOT Collect

AfterFlow AI explicitly does NOT collect:

  • Customer names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses
  • Customer payment or financial information
  • Product information, inventory data, or pricing details
  • Employee or merchant personal data beyond store domain
  • Browsing history, cookies, or tracking data

4. How We Use Your Data

Data collected by AfterFlow AI is used exclusively for:

  1. AI Note Analysis — Processing customer order notes via AI (DeepSeek / Gemini) to generate fulfillment tags and urgency scores
  2. Tag Writing — Applying AI-generated tags to Shopify orders via the Shopify Admin API
  3. Note Enhancement — Prepending an AI summary to the order note for merchant visibility
  4. Dashboard Display — Showing recent analysis results in the merchant dashboard

5. Data Retention

Analysis results (urgency, tags, summary) are stored in your own database and retained at your discretion. You may delete all data associated with AfterFlow AI at any time by uninstalling the application.

Per our GDPR compliance design, upon receiving a shop/redact request from Shopify, AfterFlow AI will purge all stored analysis records for the requesting store.

6. Third-Party Services

AfterFlow AI uses the following third-party AI services to analyze order notes:

  • DeepSeek (deepseek.com) — Primary AI engine for note analysis
  • Google Gemini (generativelanguage.googleapis.com) — Fallback AI engine

Customer order notes are sent to these services for real-time AI analysis. Both providers are GDPR-compliant and do not retain prompt data for training purposes.

AfterFlow AI does not share, sell, or transfer your data to any other third parties.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard transport encryption (TLS/HTTPS) for all data in transit. API credentials are stored as environment variables and are never exposed to the browser or client-side code. Your Shopify store's access token is encrypted at rest in our database.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing — Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@afterflow.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have not complied with GDPR.

9. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request disclosure of data collected about you, request deletion of your data, and opt out of the sale of personal information. AfterFlow AI does not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@afterflow.ai.

10. Children's Privacy

AfterFlow AI is not designed for or directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
